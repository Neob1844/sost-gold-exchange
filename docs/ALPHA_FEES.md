# Alpha Fee Structure

## Position Trading Fees
- Full position trade: 1% of trade value (paid by seller)
- Reward-right trade: 1% of reward value (paid by seller)
- Minimum fee: 0.01 SOST per trade

## OTC Execution Fees
- Operator-assisted: disclosed per trade before execution
- No hidden spreads
- Fee shown before confirmation

## What Is Free
- Viewing positions: free
- Creating offers: free (gas-free on SOST side)
- Cancelling offers: free

## Phase Policy
- Alpha fees are intentionally simple and transparent
- Fees may be adjusted as the system matures
- No yield promises, no guaranteed returns
